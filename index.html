About Us

Bali United Football Club is an Indonesian professional club based in Gianyar, Bali. Bali United began operations in 2014 and continues to be one of the highest tier in the Indonesian football competition, League 1. The club has a vision to grow the football industry in Indonesia through creating an ecosystem consisting of 4Cs namely the Club, Community, Corporation, and Country. Staying true to this vision, the football club launched a sports agency under the name United Creative, to always bring the GAME ON beyond it's own club.
